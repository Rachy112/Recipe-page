Creamy Steak Pasta Recipe

Thiscreamy steak pasta is comfort food at it's best, a mix of juicy steak and cheesy, garlicky pasta everyone will simply fall in love with.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 35 minutes
  • Cooking: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 rump steaks (about 1 ib/ 450g total)
  • Salt and Pepper, 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, 1 springs rosemary
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
  • 8 oz (250 g) pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Â½ cup (125 ml) low-sodium chicken stock. Parmesan, grated
  • 1 cup (250 ml) heavy cream
  • A handful of fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Seasoning: season your rump steaks generously with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add the steaks and cook for 3-4 minutes per slide until you see a brown crust.
  2. Add Ingredients: Add the butter, garlic and rosemary sprig to the pan with the steaks and spoon the melted butter over the steaks. Cook until they're done to your liking, remove the steaks and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing thinly.
  3. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, then cook your pasta. Reserve 1-2 cups of pasta water before drainig the pasta.
  4. Make pasta sauce: Start making the pasta sauce. Melt the butter in a skillet, add the chop garlic and cooked for 1 minute over medium- low heat.
  5. Simming: Simmer for 1-2 minutes, then add the heavy cream and continue to simmer for a couple of minutes until the sauce starts to thicken.
  6. Cook the creamy steak pasta: Add the cooked pasta to the pan together with the sliced steak. Add the chopped parsley and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve: Serve the creamy steak pasta immediately, with a bit of extra freshly grated parmesan on top if you like.
