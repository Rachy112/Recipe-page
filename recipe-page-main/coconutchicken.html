Coconut Chicken and shrimp Recipe

Need a quick appetizer? Serve with toothpicks..

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 35 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup lime-flavored seltzer water
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger, salt and pepper
  • 2-1/2 cups sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1-1/4 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 1 pound uncooked shrimp (31-40 per pound), peeled and deveined
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts (6 ounces each), cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • Oil for deep-fat frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste, optional
  • 1 can (8 ounces) crushed pineapple, well drained
  • 1/2 cup red pepper jelly
  • 3 tablespoons stone-ground mustard

Instructions

  1. Whisk the ingredient: In a shallow bowl, whisk together first 5 ingredients. In another shallow bowl, combine coconut and panko. Dip shrimp in batter to coat. Dip in coconut mixture, patting to help coating adhere. Repeat with chicken.
  2. Fry:In a deep cast-iron or electric skillet, heat oil to 350Â°. Fry shrimp, a few at a time, until golden brown, 3-4 minutes. Drain on paper towels. Repeat with chicken. If desired, sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the mustard: For mustard, mix together pineapple, pepper jelly and stone-ground mustard. Combine shrimp and chicken; serve with Maui mustard.
  4. Enjoy: Serve hot.
