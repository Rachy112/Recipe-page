Chicken Curry Recipe

The aromatic spices and flavors are a delight to the senses! Delicious with fresh naan and basmati rice.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour
  • Preparation: 45 minutes
  • Cooking: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 olive oil, 1 small onion, chooped, bay leaf
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablesppons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar, lemon juiced and cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain milk

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until lightly browned
  2. Stirring: Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon,bay leaf, ginger, suger and salt. Continue stirring for 2 minutes.
  3. Adding other Ingredients: Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yoghurt and coconut milk. Bring to boil reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Simmer: Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy: Serve hot, and enjoy!
