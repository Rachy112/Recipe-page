Spicy Shrimp Tacos Recipe

Spicy Shrimp tacos owe their kick to a smoky chipotle shrimp seasoning blend. coiling avocado and tangy picode gallo round out the flavor .

Preparation time

  • Total: 20 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 pound peeled, deveined large shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon shrimp seasoning and 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups thinly sliced red cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 8 (6inch) corn tortillas, warmed
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup pico de gallo

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil: Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large skillet over high heat. Sprinkle shrimp evenly with shrimp seasoning and salt
  2. Add shrimp to pan: Add the shrimp to the pan, cook, stirring often, until opaque, 3 to 4 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Cook the spicy shrimp tacos: Toss cabbage, cilantro, lime juice and the remaining 2 tablespoons oil in a medium bowl until combined.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Divide the shrimp among warm tortillas. Top with cabbage mixture, avocado and pico de gallo.
  5. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
