Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips!

Preparation time

  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 48

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredient: Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Heat the oven: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat the eggs: Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda: Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir: Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  6. Make dough: Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  8. Cooling: Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy: Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!.
